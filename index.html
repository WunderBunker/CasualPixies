Odyssey

The Trojan War is over, and the hero Odysseus attempts to return home to Ithaca. But will he and his crew be able to brave the dangers of this world, populated by beasts, monsters... and gods? Odyssey is an infinite runner set in Greek mythology, where you must maneuver your ship to dodge and shoot obstacles and enemies while controlling your sail to stay in the wind. Travel through different biomes and beware of their guardians : each has their own way of ambushing you in epic boss fights !
